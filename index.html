YouTube TV

A never ending stream of YouTube videos that match your interests.


Simply press the i button, enter the terms you'd use to search YouTube separated by commas (example: "cats, puppies, surfing"), and enjoy.


You can share what your selections are with friends by copying your URL and sending it to them.

Controls

In addition to the controls in the lower left of your screen, there are a few keyboard shortcuts.


Left Arrow Key previous video

Right Arrow Key next video

Spacebar pause video

Letter I open interests
